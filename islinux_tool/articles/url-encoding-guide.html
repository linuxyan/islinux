🔗 URL Encoding Guide

URL encoding (percent-encoding) is used to encode special characters in URLs.

Why URL Encode?

  • Spaces become %20
  • Special characters like & = ? need encoding in values
  • Non-ASCII characters must be encoded

Example

Hello World! becomes Hello%20World!
