🎨 Favicon Best Practices

A well-designed favicon helps users identify your site in browser tabs and bookmarks.

Recommended Sizes

  • 16×16: Standard browser tab icon
  • 32×32: High-DPI displays and bookmarks
  • 48×48: Desktop shortcuts
  • 180×180: Apple Touch Icon for iOS

Design Tips

  • Use simple, recognizable shapes
  • Ensure good contrast between foreground and background
  • Test at small sizes for clarity
  • Consider using your brand's primary color
