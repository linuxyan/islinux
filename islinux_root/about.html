About isLinux

isLinux.com is a comprehensive technical resource platform for developers and IT professionals. Our mission is to help developers improve their productivity by providing practical tools, detailed tutorials, and objective reviews.

Founded by a group of open-source enthusiasts, isLinux was born out of a need for clean, ad-balanced, and privacy-respecting developer utilities.

Our Services

We utilize a specialized subdomain matrix to ensure each toolset operates in a clean, isolated environment, providing the fastest loading speeds for our global users.

Main Site (islinux.com) - Linux command reference, tech blog, VPS reviews

AI Subsite (ai.islinux.com) - AI prompt library designed for developers

Tools Subsite (tool.islinux.com) - Pure frontend developer tools, data processed locally

Calculator Subsite (calc.islinux.com) - Financial and efficiency calculation tools

Our Commitment

  • Privacy First - All tools run in your browser locally, no data uploaded
  • High Performance - Powered by enterprise-grade servers in Singapore, ensuring low-latency access for the Asia-Pacific and global developer community
  • Content is King - Provide original, in-depth, valuable technical content
  • Open Source - Most resources are freely available to the community
