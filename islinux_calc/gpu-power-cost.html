💡 Cost Optimization Tips

  • Undervolting your GPU can reduce power consumption by 15-25% with minimal performance loss
  • Running during off-peak hours (if you have time-of-use pricing) can save 30-50%
  • Consider a dedicated electricity meter to track GPU costs separately
  • Efficient GPUs like RTX 5060 may save $100+/year vs power-hungry options
