Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 17, 2026

The isLinux Calc Data Privacy Guarantee: At isLinux Calc, we prioritize the confidentiality of your engineering and financial estimates. Our calculators are designed for transparent, local-first processing, ensuring your strategic data remains private.

1. Calculation Data Privacy

All input parameters used in our calculators—such as GPU specifications, infrastructure costs, or project timelines—are processed locally in your browser. We do not transmit, log, or store these values on our servers.

2. No Financial Tracking

We do not associate specific cost estimates or ROI calculations with your personal identity. Your business logic and resource planning remain entirely within your local session.

3. Analytics & Cookies

We collect anonymous traffic data to determine which calculation tools are most frequently used. This helps us prioritize new feature development without compromising individual user privacy.

4. Global Policy & Compliance

For more details on Google AdSense integration, third-party cookies, and global privacy standards (GDPR/CCPA), please visit our Global Privacy Policy.
